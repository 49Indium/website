--- layout: post title: Board Games Night ---

We know that people like to play games. To complement our Staff vs Students Video Games series, the UQ Computing Society is introducing a new way to have fun: Board Games Nights!

Our inaugural night of board games will be on Tuesday 12 August. We will start off with FREE PIZZA for dinner, then move on to playing games. We will have a range of games available, and you are encouraged to bring along games that you would like to play on the night. Invite your friends to come along and join in - the more the merrier.

Preceding the board games will be our Intro to Git seminar, so be sure to come along to both for an evening of learning and fun.

What: Board Games and Pizza
When: Tuesday 12th August, 5pm
Where: ITEE Learning Centre, GP South (78-217)
Why: Have some fun with your friends and other UQCS Members by playing some board games.
