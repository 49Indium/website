Intro to LaTeX Slides

One of the most requested topics for seminars has been LaTeX, and we are only too happy to deliver. If you are not aware, LaTeX is a document formatter and markup language. It allows you to focus on the content, and will automatically turn that into beautiful documents and presentations. So if you are working on a large written project (hint: a thesis), or want to find out how all those maths assignments are written, this is your chance to learn.

We are privileged to have Dr Joel Fenwick coming along to present this seminar. He has a great deal of experience using LaTeX, and will show you how to get started creating your own document masterpieces.

Where: ITEE Learning Centre, GP South (78-217)
Why: Learn how to make your own documents using a LaTeX, to impress all those lecturers!
