Accenture is coming to UQ to speak to students about their graduate program, Accenture Adventure. This is a great opportunity to find out more about Accenture and the industry. Following the seminar, a free pizza lunch will be provided to all in attendance.

What: Accenture Adventure Seminar
When: Monday 8th September, 1-2pm
Where: ITEE Learning Centre, GP South (78-217)
Why: Find out about Accenture Adventure, followed by FREE PIZZA.

What is Accenture?

With approximately 289,000 people working in 120 countries across the world, Accenture is a truly global company. The work we do ranges from important to mission critical, from interesting to career-defining. It’s important work, undertaken by passionate, dedicated people with a singular focus on improving the way things work. A career with Accenture will take you almost anywhere, working with some of the biggest and best brands in the world, guided by individual mentoring programs and support throughout, and backed up by world-class training and development opportunities everywhere.

What Programs are on now?

Accenture Adventure!

Accenture Adventure is an event held in Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, Canberra and Brisbane. The full-day event will assess participants’ ability to work within teams together with Accenture Managers and Senior Managers to overcome real business challenges. The day is concluded with an interview with Accenture Leadership for which successful participants will be offered a role within Accenture’s Graduate Program! Events will be held in February 2015. Applications open September 1st 2014 close September 30th

For more information, please see http://accenture.com/grads
