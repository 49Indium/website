--- layout: post title: 2015 Executive Nominations ---

The University of Queensland Computing Society will be accepting nominations for Office Bearers on the Management Committee for 2015. All current members of the University of Queensland Computing Society may nominate for a position.

Please read the Position Descriptions for the Management Committee before nominating.

Elections of Office Bearers for the Management Committee for UQCS will take place at the Annual General Meeting, to be held on October 27, 2014 from 5pm to 6pm, in room 78-217 at the University of Queensland, St Lucia campus. If you have any questions or comments, please mail secretary@uqcs.org.au.

Nominations are now closed and the new executive has been elected.
